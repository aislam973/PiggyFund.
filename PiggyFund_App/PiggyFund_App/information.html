Investment Pathway Overview

Equities

Invest in equities- stocks, mutual funds, or ETFs which has high return and is less risky. However, this is still risk as a company could go out of business, leading to the stocks losing its value. When investing in equities, it is important to find a diversified portfolio or mutual fund to invest in as that minimizes risk. In addition, you should find the lowest expense ratio (the amount of the fund's assets that are used for administative and operating expenses) on your mutual funds or ETF

Bonds and Treasury Securities

Bonds are issued by government and corporations when you lend them money. You can hold the bonds until thei maturity date and collect interest payments on them or sell the bonds to make a profit. Treasury securities are government bonds that pay a fixed rate of interest every six months until the security matures, which is when Treasury pays the par value.

Stock Options

Stock options are contracts that allow you to buy and sell a stock. Options are popular as you can invest with little money and get large returns, however they are a riskier way of investing in the stock market because you can lose oyur entire premium if the stock does not increase in price over time or the stock decreases in price.

Real Estate

Historically, investing in real estate requires alot of money, however in the last several years, crowdfunded real estate has loweredd the bar to entry as little as $1000. Crowfunding allows you to join other investors to pool your money together and invest in a property. Platforms such as RealtyMogul and Fundrise allows to you to easily start investing.
